Who We Are

First Class Flooring is a hardworking, family-driven flooring company serving Southwest Missouri. With years of hands-on experience, we provide top-tier flooring installations, repairs, and custom solutions with a commitment to excellence. Our focus is simple: deliver reliable, professional work that stands the test of time.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring quality craftsmanship, dependable service, and honest communication to every flooring project we take on. We believe in treating your home or business with the same care we give our own.

Our Values

  • Reliability: We show up, work hard, and get the job done right.
  • Craftsmanship: Every detail matters — we don’t cut corners.
  • Integrity: Fair pricing, honest recommendations, no surprises.
  • Respect: Your space is treated with professionalism and care.

Why Choose Us

  • High-quality installation backed by years of experience

  • Affordable and fair pricing

  • Flexible scheduling and efficient turnaround times
